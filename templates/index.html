Fake News Detection

A fake news detection model is a machine learning model that is trained to identify and classify news articles or other forms of content as either real or fake. Fake news refers to deliberately misleading, false or biased information presented as news or journalism, with the aim of manipulating public opinion or spreading propaganda.
